ChronoQuest: The Lost Timepieces

Act 6: The End

The final Timepiece clicks into place. The chaotic swirl of time begins to calm, and the timeline reasserts itself. The future, past, and present merge back into their proper order, and the fractures of reality seal themselves.

As you step back from the Nexus, a voice echoes through the temporal corridors—Dr. Elara Finch. “You’ve done it. Time is restored, but the journey is not over. More secrets of the Timepieces remain to be uncovered. In the folds of history, there are still echoes that have yet to be heard. Perhaps, one day, you will return to them.”
