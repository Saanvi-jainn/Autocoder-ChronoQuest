ChronoQuest: The Lost Timepieces

Act 4: The Cyberpunk Future

Time bends once more, thrusting you forward into a neon-lit, dystopian future. The world you enter is unrecognizable—towering skyscrapers loom over the streets, glowing under the control of a powerful AI. The third Timepiece is hidden somewhere within this futuristic city, its location concealed by the AI dictator that rules over every facet of life.

To retrieve this Timepiece, you must outwit the AI's surveillance systems. Navigate through the dark alleys, where robotic patrols hunt for intruders. The city's mainframe holds the key to unlocking the Timepiece, but the security systems are impenetrable—until now. You must hack into the AI’s core, solving intricate logic puzzles to bypass its defenses and uncover the Timepiece before the city’s enforcers close in.
